Frequently Asked Questions

Nurse Hub is an online platform that connects healthcare facilities with freelance nurses. It allows healthcare providers to find qualified nurses and gives nurses the flexibility to choose their working hours.

To sign up as a nurse, visit our registration page, fill in the required details, and submit your application. After your credentials are verified, you’ll gain access to available shifts at healthcare facilities.

Once registered, you can view available shifts in your area. Our platform allows you to filter job opportunities based on your preferences, including location, time, and type of healthcare facility.

To work as a nurse on Nurse Hub, you must have a valid nursing license and relevant professional certifications. You’ll also need to undergo a brief verification process to ensure your qualifications are up to date.

Healthcare facilities can register on Nurse Hub as employers. After registration, they can post job listings specifying the details of the shifts they need to fill, including the skills and experience required.

Nurse Hub offers both free and premium membership options. Nurses can sign up for free to access basic features, while healthcare facilities may opt for a premium plan for additional features like priority job posting and enhanced visibility.

You can reach our support team via the "Contact Us" page on our website. We provide assistance for both nurses and healthcare facilities, including technical support and inquiries about using the platform.
